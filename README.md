# ET AI Hackathon 2026: Story Arc Tracker (PS #8)

## 🎯 The Vision
Our solution transforms fragmented business news into an **AI-Native Interactive Narrative**. Instead of reading isolated articles, users follow a continuous "Story Arc" that connects MCA filings, SEBI records, and market sentiment.

## 🏗️ System Architecture
The system utilizes a **Multi-Agent Orchestrator** as detailed in our Architecture PDF:
* **Ingestion Agent**: Scrapes real-time data from NSE, SEBI, and MCA.
* **Timeline Agent**: Automatically maps narrative pivot points.
* **Sentiment Agent**: Tracks shifts in market mood.
* **Synthesis Agent**: Generates "Contrarian Insights" to highlight what the consensus is missing.

## 💰 Business Impact
Based on our **Impact Model**, this tool provides:
* **Efficiency**: Saves ~1.5 hours per analyst daily.
* **Cost Savings**: Annual savings of **₹55 Lakh – ₹78 Lakh** for a 50-analyst team.
* **Productivity**: 1,300 to 1,650 hours saved monthly.
